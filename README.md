# Escape the Goon

Escape the Goon is a small retro-style horror game made in Java.  
The game is inspired by old first-person corridor horror games with limited visibility and simple movement mechanics.

## Story

You stayed too long at work and still have not pushed your commits.

Even though you were repeatedly told to push your commits regularly, you always waited until the very end of the day.

Then the power suddenly goes out.

The building is completely dark, your work is at risk of being lost, and somewhere in the hallways, The Goon is still in the building.

Your objective is to restore the power, push your commits before your work is lost, and escape before The Goon finds out.

## Features

- Retro-inspired first-person horror gameplay
- Dark hallway exploration
- Limited visibility
- Player movement and turning
- Objective-based gameplay
- Enemy encounters
- Self-made sprites
- Simple sprite animations
- Win and lose screens
- Score system

## Gameplay

The player explores a dark office building while avoiding The Goon.

To complete the game, the player must:
1. Restore the power
2. Reach the workstation
3. Push the commits
4. Escape the building

If The Goon catches the player before the commits are pushed, the game is over.

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|------|--------|
| W / Arrow Up | Move forward |
| A / Arrow Left | Turn left |
| D / Arrow Right | Turn right |
| ESC | Quit game |
